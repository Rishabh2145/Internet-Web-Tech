About Us

Welcome to the official portal of the Electricity Department of India — your one-stop destination for all electricity-related services. We are committed to delivering reliable, transparent, and user-friendly solutions for citizens across the country.

Whether you need to pay your electricity bill, apply for a new connection, lodge a complaint, or connect with our departmental staff, our platform is designed to make every process smooth and efficient.

Our mission is to empower every household and business with uninterrupted power and trustworthy service. With a focus on digital transformation and customer satisfaction, we aim to bridge the gap between citizens and the power infrastructure that fuels their lives.

Thank you for trusting us. Together, let's light up a brighter India.
