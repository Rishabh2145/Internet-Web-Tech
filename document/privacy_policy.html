Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you interact with the Department of Electricity website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal information (e.g., name, email) provided during service registration.
  • Usage data including IP address, browser type, and access times.
  • Feedback or complaints submitted through our online forms.

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and manage electricity-related services.
  • Respond to queries and complaints.
  • Improve our digital platforms.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Data Protection

We implement adequate security measures to protect your data. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

4. Third-Party Disclosure

Your data is not sold or shared with third parties unless required by law or for service delivery with authorized entities.

5. Cookies

Cookies may be used to enhance your experience. You may disable cookies in your browser settings if desired.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Please review this page regularly for the latest information.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at contact@power.gov.in.
